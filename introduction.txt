With the increasing number of car driven on the road, car accident has been one of the most frequent accident in our daily life. Predicting severity of accidents have been very important. For people who sufferred from the accident, they can take corresponding operation based on the severity of the accident. For people who came across the traffic jam caused by the accident, they can decide whether they should change another way or you can wait for it depending on the prediction. Severity prediction can also warn drivers in advance based on the road condition, weather and other factors to avoid the occurence of accidents.
